A Purpose-Built Platform

Powerful tools designed for each stakeholder in the carbon market.

NGO Dashboard

A mobile-first interface designed for the field. NGOs can easily upload geo-tagged photos and videos of planted trees, along with species and date information. The dashboard includes a real-time counter for pending and issued credits, and simplifies reporting to stakeholders and verifiers, reducing administrative burdens.

Corporate Dashboard

An advanced analytics dashboard where companies can manage their portfolio of carbon credits. Users can view their total emissions offset, track the impact of their investments, and explore the specific reforestation projects they are supporting. This provides a transparent, auditable trail for CSR reporting.

Credit Marketplace

A transparent and secure marketplace where companies can browse, purchase, and retire carbon credits from a variety of verified projects. Each credit is tied to rich data, including project location, tree species, and verification reports, empowering buyers to make informed decisions that align with their values.
